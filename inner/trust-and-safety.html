TRUST AND SAFETY

We take our responsibility as Mintebi’s stewards very seriously. It’s our job to provide a system deserving your trust - by proactively screening for potential problems, by investigating issues brought to us by our community, and by still being exceedingly clear that even with these steps not every project will go as planned.

Our goal is to provide a safe and trusted platform where both clients and experts are comfortable about the collaboration that drive growth and value across the business spectrum.

What everyone should know

  • Even though Mintebi review all experts who apply to join the community, experts are responsible for their own deliveries. Whether the consultation takes the form of a telephone interview or a report, it is ultimately up to the individual expert to deliver to the satisfaction of the client
  • Any information disclosed as part of a project, including information about participating clients and experts, is strictly confidential, on a “need-to-know” basis, and cannot be shared by a user to any third parties
  • Mintebi treats information relating directly or indirectly to individual clients and their activity on the platform as strictly confidential and it will under no circumstances be shared externally

What experts can do

  • To participate in a project, you must consider yourself to be qualified with your expert background to sufficiently fulfil the scope set out in the project description and deliver a satisfactory consultation as requested
  • You undertake all projects to the best of your skill, and you will at all times stay within the parameters of the project as outlined in the project description
  • You will keep your profile information accurate, complete and always up to date

What clients can do

  • Be as specific as possible in describing your project. The better you prepare the expert for the task, the better the delivery, and the higher the chances of you getting many qualified pitches
  • Even though our experts are generally very good at knowing their own limits, you should always take the time to checkout the expert’s profile before accepting a pitch
  • When deciding on your project’s budget keep in mind that very senior experts typically have a higher reservation price, and that a higher budget (non-surprisingly) will generally lead to more qualified pitches
  • Remember to review experts after each delivery. The rating and review system is critical to create transparency and promote superior performance among our experts
  • Your identity is not disclosed with your project on the market place. If you check the “keep me anonymous” option when creating your project, you will also remain anonymous to the experts you accept for the project
  • When creating a project you should reserve the more sensitive information for the long project description, which is only disclosed in a private forum to the experts who work on your project

What we do

  • We listen. We didn’t just build Mintebi - we are carefully tending to it 24/7. Our integrity team is always watching over the platform and reviewing any reports from the community. Our community helps us make sure this is the safest, most effective platform around.
  • We monitor the system. We use complex algorithms and automated tools to identify and investigate suspicious activity on projects.
  • We take action. Sometimes that just means asking someone to fix a problem. But if we find users or projects that abuse the system, we don’t hesitate to suspend them.

Mintebi is an online power-house for conducting strategic research across the entire business spectrum, and in general project’s on the platform are executed with excellence. We hope you enjoy getting involved - and if there’s anything at all you need to know, just ask.
